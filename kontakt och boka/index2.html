MALMBERG WORLD

Malmberg is an international cleantech company.
We provide municipalities and industry with water
treatment, biogas, geothermal energy, drilling and waste handling.
Our production and headquarter is located in Åhus near Kristianstad,
Sweden. Malmberg is a family owned business in operation since 1866 and works for a better
environment under the motto "Pure energy. Clean water."
